The Woodshop

Andrew Carroll is 24 years old. He grew up in Mason, Ohio, where he went to high school. He attended college at the University of Cincinnati as a part of the DAAP program. In 2020, he completed his certification in Phlebotomy from Butler Tech. He currently works at Panda Express but has held several jobs in the past as a server at restaurants such as iHop and Applebees. Andy was in boy scouts when he was a youth. He currently enjoys watching movies, playing his guitar, and working in his woodshop.
